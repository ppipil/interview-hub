## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Interview page SHALL use a moderated typography scale

The interview page SHALL present its header, question content, guided hint, and answer composer with a moderated type scale that feels mobile-first and less oversized than the current version.

#### Scenario: User enters an active interview

- **WHEN** the interview page renders on the mobile-first layout
- **THEN** the page SHALL keep the current content hierarchy
- **AND** the brand title, question heading, hint text, textarea text, and action labels SHALL render at a calmer size than before
