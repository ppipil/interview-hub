## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Selected interviewer options SHALL remain readable

The setup screen SHALL keep interviewer option text visibly readable after an interviewer is selected.

#### Scenario: User selects an interviewer

- **WHEN** the user activates an interviewer option
- **THEN** the selected option SHALL maintain sufficient contrast between its background and visible text label

### Requirement: Home CTA SHALL match the app's primary action presence

The home screen SHALL present a primary CTA with enough visual width and prominence to match the other major action buttons used in the MVP flow.

#### Scenario: User lands on the home screen

- **WHEN** the minimal home entry is displayed
- **THEN** the primary button SHALL appear as a clearly prominent CTA rather than a narrow text-sized button
